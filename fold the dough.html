• Fold the Dough
    • 
01  Gather the ingredients.
		
02  Prepare a large, smooth surface for working with dough;
     a large plastic tray or a clean counter is fine.
     Spread a little vegetable oil over the work surface so dough doesn't stick.    
		
03  Keeping hands and dough well-oiled,
     divide msemen dough into smooth balls. Try the Moroccan method:
     Grasp a large portion of dough and squeeze off a ball between thumb and forefinger.
     It takes a little practice but is an efficient way to control the size of the balls.    
  		
 04  Set balls of dough on an oiled surface,
     such as a baking sheet. Be sure dough is well-oiled so it doesn't dry out.
     If making a large quantity, consider covering dough loosely with plastic while working.
     Allow dough to rest for 10 minutes.    
 		
 05  Use oiled hands to flatten and spread dough into a very thin circle or irregular square.
     Make it as thin as possible without breaking dough.
     A small hole or two is okay; they'll be concealed when the dough is folded.    
 		
06  fold one side of sough into the center.
		
07  fold opposite side into center to make a narrow strip pf dough
		
08  fold one end of the strip of dough to the center
		
09  fold opposite end of dough to center to make a square.
		
10  set folded dough aside on an oiled surface.
    repeat process untill all of the dough has been folded into squares.
